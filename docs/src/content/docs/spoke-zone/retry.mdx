## Policy

- Applies to all auth and endpoint calls in the Spoke.Zone client.
- Retry on transport errors, `429`, and `5xx`.
- Do not retry `4xx` responses other than `429`.

## Backoff

- Fixed delay schedule: `15s`, then `30s`, then `60s`.
- Same schedule applies to `DeviceAuth.login` and `UserAuth.login`.
- Shared helper types: `BackoffStrategy` with default `FixedDelayBackoffStrategy`.

## Exhaustion

- If all retries fail, surface a typed SDK error for retry exhaustion.
- Include last-attempt diagnostics in the surfaced error.
